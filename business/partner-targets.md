# Partner Targets

## Integration Roadmap

### 1. GitHub
- **Rationale**: Essential for version control and project collaboration.
- **Free-tier limits**: Unlimited public repos, 500MB storage per repo.
- **Integration effort**: S (APIs well-documented).
- **Value-add**: Enables users to save and share their coding projects, solving the job of project management and collaboration.

### 2. Notion
- **Rationale**: Popular for note-taking and project organization.
- **Free-tier limits**: Unlimited pages and blocks, 5MB file upload limit.
- **Integration effort**: M (APIs available but may require customization).
- **Value-add**: Allows users to organize their learning materials and notes, solving the job of information organization.

### 3. Discord
- **Rationale**: Community building and real-time communication.
- **Free-tier limits**: Unlimited servers, 50 users per server.
- **Integration effort**: S (APIs well-documented).
- **Value-add**: Provides a community space for users to ask questions and share knowledge, solving the job of community support.

### 4. Codecademy
- **Rationale**: Complementary learning platform with structured courses.
- **Free-tier limits**: Basic courses available for free.
- **Integration effort**: L (APIs may require negotiation).
- **Value-add**: Offers additional learning resources and structured courses, solving the job of comprehensive learning.

### 5. Replit
- **Rationale**: Online IDE for coding practice.
- **Free-tier limits**: Basic features available for free.
- **Integration effort**: M (APIs available but may require customization).
- **Value-add**: Provides an online coding environment, solving the job of coding practice and experimentation.

### 6. Zapier
- **Rationale**: Automation of workflows.
- **Free-tier limits**: 100 tasks per month.
- **Integration effort**: M (APIs available but may require customization).
- **Value-add**: Automates repetitive tasks, solving the job of workflow efficiency.

### 7. Slack
- **Rationale**: Professional communication and collaboration.
- **Free-tier limits**: 10,000 messages, 10 integrations.
- **Integration effort**: S (APIs well-documented).
- **Value-add**: Facilitates professional communication and collaboration, solving the job of team coordination.

### 8. AWS Educate
- **Rationale**: Access to cloud computing resources.
- **Free-tier limits**: Free access to AWS services for educational purposes.
- **Integration effort**: L (APIs may require negotiation).
- **Value-add**: Provides access to cloud computing resources, solving the job of hands-on cloud experience.

## Prioritization
1. **GitHub** (High priority due to essential nature and ease of integration).
2. **Discord** (High priority for community building).
3. **Notion** (Medium priority for organization).
4. **Replit** (Medium priority for coding practice).
5. **Zapier** (Medium priority for automation).
6. **Slack** (Medium priority for professional communication).
7. **Codecademy** (Low priority due to potential negotiation complexity).
8. **AWS Educate** (Low priority due to potential negotiation complexity).
