 # marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
1-line: Tech-Onramp - Empowering Newcomers to Master Tech Fundamentals and AI Tools with AI-Assisted Learning.

Alternatives:
1. Tech-Onramp Pro - Premium AI-Assisted Learning for Rapid Tech Mastery.
2. Tech-Onramp Lite - Essential AI-Assisted Learning for Tech Newcomers.
3. Tech-Onramp Plus - Comprehensive AI-Assisted Learning with Personalized Mentorship.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
### Persona 1: Sarah Johnson, Junior Developer, Budget: $50/month
- Daily Job: Writing code for web applications, learning new technologies, and collaborating with team members.
- Pain Points: Struggling with AI tools, lack of understanding of tech fundamentals, and difficulty keeping up with the fast-paced tech industry.

### Persona 2: John Kim, AI Engineer, Budget: $100/month
- Daily Job: Designing, developing, and maintaining AI models for various applications.
- Pain Points: Difficulty explaining complex AI concepts to non-technical team members, time-consuming manual processes, and the need for a deeper understanding of tech fundamentals.

### Persona 3: Emily Davis, Data Analyst, Budget: $75/month
- Daily Job: Analyzing large datasets, creating data visualizations, and making data-driven decisions.
- Pain Points: Lack of understanding of AI tools, difficulty working with unstructured data, and the need for a stronger foundation in tech fundamentals.

## Channels
1. LinkedIn Ads (CAC estimate: $50) - Targeted ads to tech professionals, including developers, data analysts, and AI engineers.
2. Content Marketing (CAC estimate: $20) - Blog posts, videos, and webinars showcasing the benefits of Tech-Onramp and addressing common pain points.
3. Referral Program (CAC estimate: $10) - Encourage existing users to refer friends and colleagues, offering incentives for successful referrals.

## Content Cadence
Week 1:
- Launch introductory blog post and video explaining Tech-Onramp and its benefits.
- Begin LinkedIn ad campaign targeting ICP personas.

Week 2:
- Publish a series of blog posts addressing common pain points and showcasing Tech-Onramp's features.
- Host a webinar featuring a Tech-Onramp expert discussing the platform and answering questions.

Week 3:
- Share success stories and testimonials from early adopters.
- Begin email marketing campaign to engage leads and encourage sign-ups.

Week 4:
- Launch referral program, offering incentives for successful referrals.
- Continue content marketing efforts, focusing on addressing specific pain points for each ICP persona.

## Launch Milestones
- D-30: Finalize content marketing materials, begin LinkedIn ad campaign, and launch referral program.
- D-0: Officially launch Tech-Onramp, begin email marketing campaign, and host webinar.
- D+30: Evaluate user feedback, make necessary adjustments to platform, and continue content marketing efforts.
- D+90: Assess DAU and $MRR targets, adjust marketing strategy as needed, and plan for future product improvements.

## Success Metrics
- DAU (Daily Active Users) - Track the number of daily active users to measure platform engagement.
- $MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) - Set a target for $MRR by D+90 to measure the financial success of Tech-Onramp.
